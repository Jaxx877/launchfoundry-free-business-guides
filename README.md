## Responsible-use standard

- Use authentic customer research and real work evidence.
- Get permission before publishing identifiable customer information or property.
- Do not purchase reviews, followers, clicks, or endorsements.
- Do not mass-message scraped contacts.
- Do not claim guaranteed revenue or automatic customers.
- Follow the rules of each community and disclose your affiliation when recommending your own work.
