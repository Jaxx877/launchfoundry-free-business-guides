# 7-Day Customer Visibility Sprint

Use this sprint to create qualified attention around one clear offer. Do one useful action per day, record what happened, and improve the next action from evidence instead of guessing.

## Before day one

Write down:

- The specific person you help
- The costly or frustrating problem they already recognize
- The useful result your offer supports
- One believable reason to trust your approach
- One low-friction next step

## Day 1 — Customer language

Review five real questions, calls, emails, community discussions, or search suggestions from the intended buyer. Record their exact wording. Do not invent pain points.

## Day 2 — One useful answer

Publish a concise answer to the most repeated question. Give the reader a complete first step before mentioning your offer.

## Day 3 — Relevant outreach

Send five individual messages to people you genuinely know or can help. Reference their situation and invite a conversation. Do not use scraped lists or mass messaging.

## Day 4 — Proof and process

Show a real workflow, example, checklist, before-and-after decision, or anonymized lesson. Get permission before sharing identifiable customer information.

## Day 5 — Partner path

Contact two complementary businesses, creators, communities, or newsletters whose audience overlaps with yours. Propose a useful collaboration instead of asking for a generic promotion.

## Day 6 — Search path

Improve one page around a specific customer question. Make the title, opening answer, headings, examples, internal links, and next step clear.

## Day 7 — Follow-up and review

Follow up with every genuine response. Record visits, replies, useful conversations, qualified inquiries, checkout starts, and purchases. Keep the channels that created relevant interest; replace the ones that did not.

## Sprint scorecard

At the end of the week, record:

- Useful pieces published
- Individual conversations started
- Partner conversations started
- Qualified website visits
- Email subscribers
- Qualified inquiries
- Checkout starts
- Purchases
- The next experiment you will run
