# Contractor Lead Follow-Up Checklist

Use this checklist to keep real inquiries from disappearing without pressuring people who are not ready or not a fit.

## Every lead record needs

- [ ] Contact method
- [ ] Service need
- [ ] Service location or area
- [ ] Current stage
- [ ] Last interaction
- [ ] One dated next action
- [ ] One owner when multiple people handle inquiries
- [ ] Final outcome and reason

## Stage checklist

- [ ] Acknowledge the inquiry and name the expected response window.
- [ ] Collect only the missing details needed to choose the next step.
- [ ] Confirm estimate or appointment preparation.
- [ ] After the estimate, offer to clarify scope, assumptions, timing, exclusions, or options.
- [ ] Send one relevant follow-up rather than an endless generic sequence.
- [ ] Close the loop as won, lost, nurture, or not a fit.
